Integrates the differential equation using a s stage RK scheme and a rudimentary stepsize-control.

Doubles the number of timesteps until the desired accuracy is reached Checks for NaN errors on the way and if the fieldline diverges. The error is computed in the first three vector elements (x,y,s)
